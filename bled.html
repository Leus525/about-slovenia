Bled

About Bled

Bled, a Slovenian resort town in the foothills of the Julian Alps, is set along the glacial Lake Bled. On a cliff overlooking the lake is the 11th-century Bled Castle, which houses a museum, chapel and printing press. Atop a small island in the lake's center is the Pilgrimage Church of the Assumption of Maria, with its steep staircase and bell tower.

History

The dangers of Italy, which occupied Primorsko and Istro and parts of Dalmatia on the basis of the London Agreement of 1915, undefined borders, the absence of international recognition and the pressure [dubious - see the discussion page] of Serbs after the merger into the common state, resulted on December 1, 1918 the merger of the State of SHS with the Kingdom of Serbia into the Kingdom of Serbs, Croats and Slovenes, which, since 3 October 1929, was called the Kingdom of Yugoslavia. In 1919, Yugoslav troops occupied Prekmurje and joined the kingdom. After the lost Carinthian plebiscite in 1920, southern Carinthia became Austria. After the signing of the Treaty of Rapallo (1920), a border with Italy was established, which received the whole of western Slovenia. On 1 November 1918, Major Rudolf Maister proclaimed Maribor and the Lower Styria for the possession of the State of SHS and took over the military command over Maribor and all of Lower Styria to ensure the belonging of Lower Styria to Slovenia in the barracks of the 26th Regiment's Regiment in Melju, in front of the City Commander, Lieutenant Colonel Anton Holik.

Let's go around Bled!

Summer Bobsleing

If you would like to spice up your visit of Bled with a touch of adrenaline, than you must visit Straža in summer. From spring to autumn you can take a ride on the sledding track that is situated on the ski slope along the chair lift.

Сable park on Bled

Family Adventure Park Bled is located on the peak of the skiing area, Straža Bled. Walk along Riklijeva pot. This brings you over the ski slope to the top of Straža Hill. Takes 20 minutes.

SUP Bled

Voted the “Best summer tour”, sup tour takes you away from the crowds and shows you how relaxing and fun paddle boarding experience can be!

Bled viewing platform

Mala Osojnica is a view point located 645 metres above Lake Bled and is our favourite shooting location. A lot of people will tell you that Slovenia’s Lake Bled is a piece of genuine fairytale magic.

The Vintgar gorge

This ravine in the immediate vicinity of Gorje, approximately 4 km north-west of Bled, was discovered by Jakob Žumer, the Major of Gorje, and the cartographer and photographer Benedikt Lergetporer in 1891.

Bled castle

BLED CASTLE – according to written sources, the oldest castle in Slovenia, first mentioned in a 1011 donation deed as castellum Veldes. It has been over a thousand years since.
